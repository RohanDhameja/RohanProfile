Musical Journey

"Music is the universal language that connects hearts across cultures. Through piano and sitar, I explore the beautiful intersection of Western and Eastern musical traditions."

My Musical Timeline

Age 6 - 2010

First Piano Lesson

My musical journey began when my grandmother gifted me a small keyboard. The magic of creating melodies instantly captivated me, setting the foundation for a lifelong passion.

Age 12 - 2016

First Public Performance

Performed Chopin's "Minute Waltz" at my school's talent show. The encouraging applause from my peers gave me confidence to pursue music more seriously.

Age 15 - 2019

Discovering the Sitar

My grandfather introduced me to the sitar, connecting me with my Indian heritage. Learning this ancient instrument opened a new world of ragas and Eastern musical philosophy.

Age 17 - 2021

Cultural Fusion Concert

Organized my first fusion performance, blending Western classical piano with traditional Indian ragas. This concert raised funds for music education in underserved communities.

Present Day - 2024

Teaching & Sharing

Now teaching music to kids in my community and creating YouTube content that bridges Eastern and Western musical traditions, inspiring others to explore diverse cultures through music.

Why Music Matters to Me

Music has taught me that diversity creates beauty. Just as Western classical harmony complements Eastern melodic traditions, our differences in the tech world can create something more beautiful than what we could achieve alone.

Cultural Bridge

Through music, I've learned to navigate between two rich cultural traditions, finding harmony in their differences and strength in their union.

Patient Learning

Both piano and sitar have taught me patience, discipline, and the importance of celebrating small progress—lessons I apply to my work with neurodiverse students.
